Are you cringing at the thought of painstakingly scrubbing and scraping in order to cut through encrusted food and grease that lives on your grill? With our revolutionary grill cleaning system you can melt away the grease and grime with all natural hot steam. Put our grill steam cleaner to work at restoring your grill to its original radiance.

Put an End To Grimy Grills The Grilltastic barbecue cleaner will thoroughly clean grills of any size and type - gas, charcoal, propane, electric, and smokers alike. With a sleek design and a stainless steel brush attachment, you can clean even the hardest to reach corners. There is an easy, anytime refill, with no cool down time required for refilling. A 16.5 foot cord allows for extended reach.

The Perfect Gift For Any Occasion The Grilltastic steam grill cleaner makes a great birthday, holiday, or special gift for the grill master that has everything. The Grilltastic will be a welcome accessory for the seasoned pitmaster or the novice griller flipping their first burger.

Simplicity Meets Efficiency Throw away those rusty wire brushes and harmful chemicals and try our simple solution to this nasty, foreboding chore. With the press of a button you will control a direct, cleansing steam which will melt away even the most stubborn buildups. The steam option has an on/off mode and can be used on a hot or cold grill.

Go Beyond the Grill Compatible with the optional Grilltastic accessory kit that allows for a wide range of cleaning options. Attachments efficiently clean additional outdoor surfaces.
